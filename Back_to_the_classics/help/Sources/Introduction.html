Back to the Classics

Welcome to Back to the Classics, the ultimate gaming experience that takes you on a nostalgic journey to the golden age of video games.


Game Description:

Back to the Classics features a 7x7 game board where you must defend your territory against an invasion of classic video game characters. Your objective is to strategically eliminate these invaders and prevent them from taking over your entire territory.

Gameplay:

  • Play: Engage in thrilling battles against waves of invaders as you strategize and eliminate them to protect your territory.
  • Quit: Exit the game and return to the main menu.
  • Shop: Visit the in-game shop to upgrade your defenses and acquire powerful weapons to aid you in your mission.
  • Start: Begin the invasion and test your skills against the relentless invaders.

Remember, your score will increase based on the number of invaders you eliminate, and special rewards await you for successfully defending your territory.
