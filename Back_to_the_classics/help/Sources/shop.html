Shop Description:

Welcome to the Shop, where you can choose your desired prizes using the points you have earned during the game.


How to Choose Prizes:

  • Select the prize you want from the list of available awards.
  • Add the selected prize to your basket.
  • If you change your mind, you can delete the prize from your basket.

Award Details:

The awards available in the shop include various categories such as Accessories, Consoles, and Videogames. Each award is uniquely identified by an award code and has a name, description, type, and value in points.

Example:

	Name: Turtle Beach Headset
	Description: Immerse yourself in your games with the powerful amplified sound and bass boost of the Recon 200
	Type: Accessories
	Value in Points: 150

How to Select Prizes:

  • Choose the prize(s) you desire, making sure that the total points value of your selection does not exceed your game score.
  • You can select multiple units of the same item if you wish.
  • Filter the gifts by categories to view the list of available prizes.
  • The chosen prizes and the remaining points will be visible on the interface.
  • You can make changes to your prize selection until you definitively confirm your choice.
  • When you're ready to confirm your prizes, enter your ID or identification document (no specific format validation is required). The application will notify you that you can pick up your prizes at any time.

Enjoy exploring the shop and selecting your desired prizes!
