Item Pickup Instructions:

After you have entered your ID and chosen your prizes, you can proceed to pick up your items. Follow the steps below:


  • Return to the shop from where you received the valid ticket for your prizes.
  • Present your ID or identification document to the shop staff.
  • Inform them that you have selected your prizes and would like to collect them.
  • The shop staff will assist you in retrieving your chosen items.

Make sure to keep your valid ticket and ID with you when picking up your items. Enjoy your prizes!
